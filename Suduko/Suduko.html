Instruction:

Sudoku is played on a 9x9 grid, divided into 3x3 subgrids called regions or blocks. The grid must be filled with digits from 1 to 9.

  • Once you click the digits from 1 to 9, the timer will start.

  • If you mismatch a number with the solution, it will reduce count as an error.

  • Errors will deduct from the total; if you reach 0 errors, the game ends.

  • If you do not finish the game before the timer runs out, the game ends.
